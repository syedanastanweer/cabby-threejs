About
Us

At Cabby, we believe in making transportation smarter, more affordable, and tailored to your needs. Our innovative platform combines convenient ride-hailing with targeted advertising, giving riders access to lower fares while offering businesses a unique way to reach their audience. Whether you’re looking for a cost-effective ride, a powerful advertising tool, or an opportunity to drive on your own terms, Cabby has you covered.

Driven by AI technology and a commitment to efficiency, we’re redefining urban mobility—putting savings, personalization, and convenience in the hands of riders, advertisers, and drivers alike.

What Cabby Offers for Everyone

At Cabby, we offer something for everyone. Riders benefit from affordable fares, thanks to our ad-subsidized model, along with a personalized experience tailored to their preferences and real-time ride tracking for peace of mind. Advertisers can reach their ideal audience through precision-targeted ads, supported by detailed performance insights, and enjoy multi-platform visibility both in-app and on taxi roofs. For drivers, Cabby provides flexible earnings, the freedom to drive on your own schedule, and the support of a growing community. With increased demand and transparent fares, drivers can enjoy more opportunities and a hassle-free experience.
